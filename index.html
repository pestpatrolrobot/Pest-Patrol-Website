Our Mission

Engineering a Sustainable Tomorrow

Pest Patrol was born from a singular vision: to bridge the gap between manual pesticide application and practical automation. Our system is designed as a mobile spraying car that helps farmers apply treatment more efficiently.

Efficiency

Supporting consistent spraying while reducing unnecessary pesticide use.

Reliability

Consistent performance across all terrain conditions.

Why Pest Patrol?

"In an era where food security is paramount, we focus on a practical automated pesticide car that can help protect crops with controlled movement and spraying."
